About me

Right now I am a visiting professor at École Centrale de Pékin, in China, where I teach analysis and probability in French to Chinese students.

Before that I was the first employee and R&D Lead at Ida, a French AI startup that optimizes fresh food orders for retailers. I built its whole R&D pipeline from scratch, and Ida was bought by RELEX Solutions in 2025.

On the side I run HSK Level and Chinese Grammar Gems, a platform and newsletter for learning Chinese that together reach more than 25,000 people around the world.

I did my PhD in applied mathematics and machine learning at Institut Polytechnique de Paris, working on algorithmic optimal transport with Marco Cuturi as my advisor.

Featured demo · Subspace Robust Wasserstein

A live taste of my research: Subspace Robust Wasserstein Distances (Paty & Cuturi, ICML 2019). Optimal transport measures the effort to morph one point cloud, \(\mu\), into another, \(\nu\); this demo finds the single line (yellow) along which the two clouds are hardest to align.
